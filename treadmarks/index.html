treadmarks..

Aug 23rd 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
Taking a trip down memory lane.. Its something that we all do once in a while.. but never have I woken up in the morning and gotten out of my bed only to tumble down one of those reveries.. I was going through the old website that I had made for myself a long time ago, around five years back.. some people wont even understand, I guess even websites, being so mechanical and coded can have emotional value or have that power to just pull your soul out.. It was one part of my life that I had lived out and it was not so bad.. I could really look back at it and smile.. even laugh at myself and my goof ups.. and also learn a lot from it all..

its not nostalgia, no.. I know that part is over.. and the read-only attribute that it has is what makes it even more valuable.. Nowadays I really look forward to the future.. The unknown brought with it immense room for all sorts of adventures :) which im certain I don’t mind having :)
