Bond 21

Nov 20th 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
I just came back from a nice down to earth secret agent flick.. The cars were stylish.. the stunts awesome.. the special effects wicked.. and the gadgets almost missing.. the movie was Casino Royale.. yup.. the new 007 movie!! w00t!! it was really good to see the movie series coming back to reality than relying on all the cg fx and uber cool gadgets.. the new blonde bond was also a nice change.. stylish, reckless, arrogant, egoist.. The new, young James Bond is played by Daniel Craig and he does a really good job of it.. the fast paced movie is a good one worth a watch..

And after that, I strolled back devouring my chocolate ice cream cone.. at midnight.. in the stupid november cold!! heheheh.. but it was nice.. nitez to all.. and go watch this movie.. :)
