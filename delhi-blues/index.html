Delhi blues..

Jan 29th 2007 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
Im not in a mood for anything.. kinda sad about some stuff.. well.. thats about it.. had a really nice weekend.. and woke up on sunday feeling so sad..

Went for a delhi tour again.. with all my students.. well.. friends.. :) and it was nice.. better than the last time.. we made it to all places and kept time.. hehehe.. now thats an achievement eh.. hmm.. hung out with all of them till late after coming back.. it was fun.. got to know them all even better.. it was really nice..

I saw deja vu last friday.. it was good.. worth a watch.. and the Skoar! expo '07 was good as well..

just sad that they will all go soon.. and its just sad.. i mean.. ive said this before.. but its cruel.. to lose friends like this every six months.. *sigh* just when you get to know everyone.. its time to go.. hmm.. i will go soon as well.. like we all said.. what happens in Delhi, stays in Delhi..
