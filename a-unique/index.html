a unique..

Apr 22nd 2007 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
I just went through a commendable effort in reading through an entertaining piece of fiction that was about three hundred pages long in the last four hours of my life.. no.. this blog is not about the book.. frankly its not even inspired by the book.. i just wanted to say that i can still read fast..

~

Who are you? have you ever asked yourself that? from the thousands of millions of people in the world, what makes you different? no, not on a superficial level.. but what is that makes you really unique? your ego might climb up and say "i am unique you idiot", but ask it the same question.. if you are, then why?

No, i haven't done anything that is worthy of any praise or recognition and i really don't consider fame to be one of those things that make you unique.. i guess the uniqueness comes from within.. the selfless efforts that you do on small things that satisfy you.. small or big.. it doesn't matter.. as long as u are happy doing it and is satisfied, at least for the moment..

maybe those few individuals who take the time and effort to do things like that, make them unique? im not sure.. but i guess the process would be rewarding.. and the moment of achievement and success belongs all to themselves.. and hence they are different.. and unique, even for a moment..?

~

Please contribute some of your precious time at the efforts of some of the most selfless, hardworking, crazy and dedicated gamers i've come across..
