FUBAR bright and early

Published: Aug 17th 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated: Jun 14th 2026

Well what a *pleashant* day it has been till now..

I woke up to the knocks on the door, the tiffin walah had come to take yesterdays uneaten dinner away.. The alarm on my mobile did soften me up a bit at 7:30 but this really did the trick.. I was awake.. walked slowly to my computer which was slowly chugging along.. the virus scan scheduled to run every day was eating away its memory.. as usual i cancelled it.. gmail has updated itself!! file transfer, voice mails and songs as status messages.. nice.. there was no one line to tell what had happend.. :-S

My phone bills were unusually high for the last couple of months.. i was wondering if somthing was wrong.. well i call up hutch customer care and to my utter disbelief, my hutch share service was not activated!! it was never activated!! :-S well.. this special service lets u select a number and lets u make calls to that number at 30ps per minute.. what do they tell me.. the plan that im on wont let me do that!! since they are already giving a lot of things free on this plan!! :-S fuming.. i never wanted the other shit!! i wanted the hutch share damn it!!! :-S aargh!!

you know.. when you slowly try to pull yourself up from the shit you are in, a whole new load of shit falls on your head..

a happy fucking good morning to you all.
