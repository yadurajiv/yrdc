I, w3ird..

Published: Aug 18th 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated: Jun 14th 2026

The majority of the people who dont know me.. and trust me, thats a vast majority.. and also a lot of people who know me, kinda think im weird.. how can i explain weird.. well.. weird like Hagrid :D only when u get to know me better, do you realise that this guy *is* weird but ok!! :p

If u have seen me in the last couple of months, the first thing that you are gonna notice is my hair.. all wild and with a life of its own.. no brush hath stood in its wake and lived to tell the tale!! so now u know what a mess it is.. and in all my life, i never had the *good* habit of combing my hair.. I never had so much hair to comb either, till recently.. and well.. i liked to style other ppls hair, especially girls.. :D than mine.. and i was good at it too.. =)

Since im not going to teach till 29th im stuck home; looking for projects and working towards making my game programming dreams into a much more tangible thingy.. Apart from that, there is nothing much happening in my life right now.. reading - nil.. unless u count code as reading :D as for writing, :( well, this blog is it.. nothing more..

[off-topic]
oh.. as im downloading some "sexy dingbat" fonts as the section on dafont tells me, i notice im getting around 27K download speeds.. wonderful.. the fonts!! they r fr sm logo work.. dnt even ask!!
[end off-topic]

So, since I had nothing better to do today, i decided to go to the nearest McDonalds.. No, not because i dont have a better place to go, closer to where i live.. but since i love their fries and cold coffee, the burgurs are gud if they dont put mayo on them.. :op So again.. here I am.. on my way to McD.. with my wild mane in the wind, waving at anyone who cares to look at it.. and me trying to subdue it in all way possible!!

I sit outside McD for a while, talking to a friend of mine on the phone.. while i was engrossed in the conversation, my hair was doing the conga infront of everyone.. and finally after the conga session, i walk into McD and go to the counter.. Here i am standing infront of the counter waiting to order and the counter girl has nothing better to do but stare at my head!! which i felt was getting bigger and bigger by the minute.. as soon as they served it, i grabbed my cold coffee and fries and ran to the un-occupied chair in the corner.. before i get a chance to reach there, another guy has locked on to my head!! and he(!!) is quick to point me out to his girlfriend, who is sitting next to him.. who, like people with half a brain and that too not ever used for thinking!! starts to giggle and smile.. finally after all that drama and a flying finger at the guy, i sit down..

Almost 40degrees to me, from my protective little corner, two babes are eating what seems to be dead chicken between two buns.. since they are babes i dont mind.. poor chickens though.. like the guy, who had nothing better to do, she starts staring and smiling as if i had a dead goat on my head!! which by this point i really wish i did.. 'cos that would have just made more sense.. :|

*EDIT*
what happend was.. a grl i used to work with appeared out of nowhere.. and was talking to the babes on the bench.. table.. wateva.. and so.. well.. nothing much.. seems like they were frnds.. and.. as im about to leave.. i say a hi and a bye.. and i run off..
*EDIT*

*EDIT*
and there i was about to leave.. putting away my phone and slowly getting up.. this guy, supposedly who works there, who was walking by, all of a sudden stops, turns around, walks to me and ask "girl friend nahi aaya kya" smirks and walks away.. there i am, standing, all smiles and dumb!! i guess McD is off limits till i get a hair-do and a girlfriend.. jeez..
*EDIT*

I mean.. are these people so perfect that they never had a bad hair day.. (of course, im having a bad hair lifetime.. and its not impossible to spend some money on it and rectify it) even if they are so perfect.. isnt it a bit sadistic to just laugh at someone? i wonder if they would do the same if it was their friend sibling or parent.. hmmm..

or maybe im just being a prick right now for being self conscious then.. and ranting on it cos of my hair's hurt ego.. in which case.. this blog was such a waste of time.. sorry if you read it :D

ps: i still look crappy o/ hehehehe..
