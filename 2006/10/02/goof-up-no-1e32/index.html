Goof Up No. 1.e+32

Published: Oct 2nd 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated: Jun 15th 2026

Disclaimer : You are not allowed to crticise my food habits nor are you allowed to take me up on the money I wasted buying stuff!! :D muhuhahahahaaaaa *sigh*

Ok.. so its late..

Here I am, all hungry and trying to get some well deserverd sleep.. I've been up and making icons all night yesterday and all day today.. Pumped out around 33 icons :p only to find out that 24 of them were slightly out of context!! Strange but pretty fun occupation this.. well.. not if its your only way of income.. :)) After all that, i learn that I wont be getting my lunch nor my dinner from my usual tiffin service as today is a public holiday.. :|

So here I am.. lying down.. trying to get some sleep.. and bam!! it hits me.. im hungry :| I could have slept.. and had a nice breakfast.. but no.. i had to go and order myslef a late dinner( @ 11 in the night!! ) The dinner arrives.. after more than half an hour and a phone call later.. To my horror and surprise I see this huge bag containing food!! and a small piece of paper telling me that Iam to pay a lot!! of money!! :| take my word for it.. it was a lot..

For a little while, I thought I was more of a dellite like any other and I knew thought I knew what it took to live in this town.. Never under estimate the power of the local dabba wallah.. he might pack you up with a whoppe of a meal if you dont know the menu.. and my fault was that I pretended to be a know-it-all.. The dinner was more than I could handle.. The bill was ok.. but it was a overall waste.. :( of food and money..

So I write a blog at my own expense just to make me feel better that I did goof up today like many other days.. :-S

Suddenly all this seems stupidly meaningless.. :-S
