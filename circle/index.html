The Design Circle

Philosophy

Game design is non-linear. Ideas rarely start at the theme and end at the loop. The Circle is a thinking device that allows you to enter the process anywhere; be it a core loop, a specific rule, or an intended emotion. This way of questioning works both for specific features to large games.

"A game is stable when you can walk the circle in any direction without finding a contradiction."

How to Use This Tool

  • 01 Workspace: This is your drafting table. Expand each node and document your thoughts. Use the jump-links in the sidebar to navigate quickly.
  • 02 Management: Use the sidebar to save/load individual sheets or backup your entire library. All data is stored locally in your browser.
  • 03 Print: Use the "Print Design" button in the sidebar to generate a clean PDF or paper copy of your current view.
