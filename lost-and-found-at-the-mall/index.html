Lost & Found at the Mall

Jun 20th 2021 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026

A game about working at the Lost & Found at the Mall. Your duty is to question the very fabric of existence and return the right items to the right people. But be careful! There might be thieves trying to take what isn’t theirs.

Lost & Found At The Mall was a game made for Global Game Jam 2021.
