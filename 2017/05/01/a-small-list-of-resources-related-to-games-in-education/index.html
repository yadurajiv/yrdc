A small list of resources related to games in education

Published: May 1st 2017 By Yadu Rajiv Updated: Jun 15th 2026

This list was a Google doc that I started as a resource for a 6 month long project/class, which revolved around solving the numeracy and literacy issue for a specific demographic. The list is a collection of websties, games and more which recently came back to life with a discussion on chat.gamedev.in. I thought I'd collate all those links and put it up as a blog post so that it can help me and others who are interested in games and/in education. With recent updates, I'm not being too strict about the list just having only games or only games super specific to education, but even games that can somehow be appropriated for our cause :)
