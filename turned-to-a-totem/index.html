Turned to a Totem

Nov 11th 2013 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
[gallery link="file" size="medium" ids="565,563,559,560,561,564"] Art, Design and Development - Turned to Totem is an experimental 2D side scrolling platformer for mobile platforms that explores multimodal input on the mobile devices, mainly inputs from the accelerometer and the microphone - apart from touch. A seal has been broken, an evil released, and you, turned to a totem. You must collect all the pieces of the seal and bind the evil back before the apocalypse.
