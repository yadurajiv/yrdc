Wyverndel Express

Oct 2nd 2020 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026

Wyverndell Express (Digital game) was part of Osmo's Math Wizard and the Magical Workshop – Where you operate your very own dragon courier business! Solve word problems at every station to unlock new routes.

I was the Lead Game Designer on Wyverndel Express responsible for the core gameplay mechanics, interaction and user interface elements (like the wheel input etc), store and economy design, lore, character design and more. I also prototyped a system that dynamically generated questions which were converted to synthesized speech, that was later implemented in the final game.
