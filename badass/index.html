Badass

Nov 15th 2012 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
[gallery link="file" size="medium" ids="545,546,547,542,543,544"]   Art, Design and Development - badass is a side scrolling action puzzle game where you are an agent and student of the school of badass(SOB). This game was made over the weekend for Ludum Dare 25. The number of levels in the game are considerably less. You must now set out to do some dubious deeds in order for you to graduate. You hack through computers and steal sensitive data while staying clear of death dealing blades to overzealous turrets.
