follow up..

Sep 10th 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
Like all movies this one also lets me put on my critics hat.. no.. im not gonna bore u with what i thought about the movie.. no interpretations.. but one thing that i have to say is that the contrast between the real world and that of the film is so so large that it is infact disturbing..

we live in the real world.. no.. it wont go away if you close your eyes and wish it away.. and no.. not all things get better by sleeping on it.. if you are lucky, it might..

yet another sunday.. and yet another monday on its way.. No, its not that i dont want to work.. nor is the crowd boring.. or the subject.. but i guess its not just want i want to do at the moment.. again.. another monday.. *sigh*

When we were driving back.. i was sitting so laid back, happy, the sun on my face.. and i was watching the clouds moving about lazily.. and i was telling my friends that we should go some place.. some place nice.. some place we have to drive a bit to reach to.. and relax.. dil chahta he style.. it might never happen.. but it was a happy thought anyway.. at least for two minutes, i enjoyed the drive back to my place.. i lived that wish.. i am happy..
