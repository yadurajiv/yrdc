Circulets

May 9th 2013 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
[gallery type="slideshow" size="full" link="none" ids="581,607,578,579,580,582,583,606,588,587,586,585,584,589,590,591,592,593,595,596,597,598,599,600,601,602,603,604,605"] Art, Design, Development and Porting - Circulets is a two+ player game about people and the interactions between them. Circulets aims to be an open ended medium that helps share a minute of your time with family, friends or anyone. There are no clearly defined rules in the game. You can collect coloured circles that has a predefined value. The numbers displayed in the end of each round(30, 60 or 90 seconds) can be interpreted in whatever way the players wants. As opposed to the timed round, an infinite time mode also exist, where the user needs to decide the amount of circulets to be collected. Circulets started as a game for 1GAM February 2013.
Most Innovative Game at Indie Prize Asia 2013 Circulets was awarded as the Most Innovative Game at Casual Connect Asia 2013 – a premier casual game developers conference.
3RD BEST CASUAL GAME AT BEST APP EVER 2014 Awarded third place for Best Casual Game after Candy Crush Saga and Clash of Clans at the 6th Best App Ever 2014 Android Awards.
