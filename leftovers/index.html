leftovers..

Aug 19th 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
Fed up with almost everything, she hangs up the phone, not waiting to listen to what he was shouting about.. it only echoed in her aching head making new waves in the pool of pain she was in.. She held her head with both her hands and let out a scream that would have terrified anyone who wished to hear it, her parted lips let out nothing but a deep moan and a sigh.. while her withered little mind screamed and twisted in pain.. far away from him, wanting to be rid of him, she lie in her soft warm bed, wishing she had a life of her own..
