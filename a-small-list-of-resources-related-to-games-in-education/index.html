A small list of resources related to games in education

May 1st 2017 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
This list was a Google doc that I started as a resource for a 6 month long project/class, which revolved around solving the numeracy and literacy issue for a specific demographic. The list is a collection of websties, games and more which recently came back to life with a discussion on chat.gamedev.in. I thought I'd collate all those links and put it up as a blog post so that it can help me and others who are interested in games and/in education. With recent updates, I'm not being too strict about the list just having only games or only games super specific to education, but even games that can somehow be appropriated for our cause :) Last updated on October 06, 2017 A presentation on gamification - https://www.slideshare.net/bamdone/gamification-defining-designing-and-using-it An article in the Indian Express about the glorification of ‘examination results’ and conventional teaching methods practiced by our society - http://epaper.indianexpress.com/c/8159221 About Science having no boundaries - https://www.ted.com/talks/beau_lotto_amy_o_toole_science_is_for_everyone_kids_included About society’s views towards children - https://www.ted.com/talks/adora_svitak?language=en Here is also a website/community-based platform that is useful for looking up resources regarding education technology in India - http://edtechreview.in/ Extra Credits: Games in Education (Playlist) - https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLhyKYa0YJ_5BIUqSDPmfBuKjTN2QBv9wI Game Theory's Top 10 Educational Games - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=COQVPrsTK3Y Top 5 Edutainment Games - brutalmoose feat.
