OSS Camp - Delhi

Jun 12th 2007 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
OSS Camp is an ad-hoc unconference motivated by the desire of learning by sharing. We at OSS Camp believe in the ideology of Open Source and thus create an open environment to facilitate learning at OSS Camps. These camps are intense events with discussions, presentations, and interactions from our participants. At OSS Camps, every attendee is a participant. Anyone willing to contribute or wishing to learn is welcome in to the community.

OSS Camp Delhi will be held in the city of New Delhi, the capital of India on September 8th-9th, 2007. You can most probably catch me there if you were to come down. The venue for the event is yet to be announced. You can keep track of whats going on by going to the official website of OSS Camp Delhi

Be there.. the future is open :)
