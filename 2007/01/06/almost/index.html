almost!!

Published: Jan 6th 2007 By Yadu Rajiv Updated: Jun 15th 2026

i was about to submit my dA journal entry and my firefox crashed :| crud.. :P so here i am.. writing it all over again :)

hmm.. ok.. so i had an unexpected run in with some friends whom ive been wanting to meet for a long time!! so these guys dropped in from college and that was really cool :) had a blast!! :)

we hung out till evening and left at around 7ish.. i dropped them of at the nearest auto stand and was walking back.. and all of a sudden i have this weird idea of going to this sony showroom that had newely opened.. :| i walk in, this guy comes up to me and asks me he cld help me.. so i say.. take me to your psps.. and he obliges.. :-S shit!! 350 USD :-S now what.. i have the money!! its not that.. the hardest part was walking out of there without buying it!! :P i called up my brother and told him im about to buy a new psp.. and he told me not to waste my money!! and poof!! i was back to normal and ran home before i did anything else!! i almost had it!! i was friggin holding it :P heheheh..

one of the most easiest things i've ever done in my life is spend money.. and one of the most hardest things i've done is *not* to spend money when i have it.. :| and i still suck at not spending!! :-S

oh well.. first priority is a wacom tablet.. then comes a nice camera and some play doh.. then comes a psp.. entertainment can wait!!

hmm.. what if i ran into a gypsy selling a tablet or an enchanted psp!! :D hmm.. thats another story i guess.. :P or what if sm1 gifts me a tablet *hint* *hint* :D

and what else.. i uploaded my brothers video for his song on his site.. check it out here at manurajeev.com/

thats about it for now.. im tired and im sleepy.. and crap!! i almost had it in my hands :-S
