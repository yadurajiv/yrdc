D for Deviance

Published: May 16th 2007 By Yadu Rajiv Updated: Jun 15th 2026

I dont know why.. but for the last two days I was just intrigued by Deviant Stamps from Deviant Art.. and of course i made my own.. i actually sat and made three yesterday and expirimented on another three and remade the last three and added another one to it.. so i had new four stamps.. heheh.. i dont know if it was a waste of time.. but it was fun none the less.. the only point at which i was pissed of was that when i found that the size was supposed to be 99x56(no rule or anything) but i overshot it a lot because i was using a base stamp to make my own.. and i never bothered to check the default size.. and resizing was a major pain in the ass!

anyways, that gave me some time to reflect on how i work.. i need to be a lot more careful before doing anything.. even uploading.. i had to do everything all over again there as welll.. thats that..
