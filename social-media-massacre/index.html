Social Media Massacre

Feb 17th 2020 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026

An interactive fiction made on twine. This game takes the player through anonymized political tweets and social media junk messages to remind them of the futility of social media discourses. The player realizes that meaningful conversations are not possible anymore.
