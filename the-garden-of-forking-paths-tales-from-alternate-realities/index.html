I had a chance to speak at the IGDC Dev Days in Kochi on the 30th of May, 2024. The initial idea for this talk was very different. I was trying to figure out what to talk about at the dev days event and my first impulse was to do something around practical gamedev advice which was around doing market research, planning and resources etc. - the sort of things to think about when you are starting out. But it felt really generic and impersonal - and like always I thought I try something new (this is also probably something to learn from - do try new things all the time!).
