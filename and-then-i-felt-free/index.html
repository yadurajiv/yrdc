and then.. i felt free..

Oct 22nd 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
I love music.. to put it all down into words.. its just crazy.. but im gonna try a bit.. :P

I walked about 4 miles to my house just now.. tuning into the local radio stations and just wishing that they would play some good songs!! I heard some i really loved.. like..
Breaking a Habit - Linkin Park..
Boulevard Of Broken Dreams - Greenday..
Iris - Goo Goo Dolls..

Im me.. but i am no one.. Im yours.. but you can never have me.. Im everywhere.. Im beautiful.. im ugly.. im sorry.. im feel a lot of things.. But, for that 2 minutes that pass by like mere seconds.. nothing matters, for I am truly happy and I am free..
