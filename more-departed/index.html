more departed..

Nov 7th 2006 By Yadu Rajiv Updated Jun 5th 2026
to be consumed by music is something really wonderful that can happen to anyone.. anyone with the right ears that is..

Back to the departed.. soundtrack this time.. Roger Waters, Van Morrison, The Band.. The Beach Boys, The Rolling Stones, Roy Buchanan, Dropkick Murphy are some of the names on the OST to name a few..

Dropkick Murphy's Im shipping of to Boston really did the trick for me.. The instrumental by Roy Buchanan titled Sweet Dreams is a sweet dream indeed.. Although The Rolling Stones had three songs listed, i only heard one.. ok i was not a Rolling Stones fan.. untill now i guess.. :) the song was Let it Loose.. Two really neat instrumental tracks by Howard Shore, The Departed Tango and Beacon Hill really added to the magic and drama in the movie..
